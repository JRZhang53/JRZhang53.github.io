    • Algebra 1
    • Geometry
    • Algebra 2
  • Has participated (and did fairly well) in any of the following competions:
    • Mathmasters
    • AMC8
    • AMC10
    • CML
    • MNJHML
    • Mathcounts
    • MNHML
